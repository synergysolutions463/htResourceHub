Apply Now!


If your organization provides services to victims of human trafficking and you would like to be included in our Resource Hub, fill out an application by clicking the button below. After filling in your organization's information and submitting the form, an e-mail will be sent to the Kent County Human Trafficking Task Force for review. You will be notified if your organization is approved.


If your organization is approved, you will need to update your organization with the Kent County Human Trafficking Task Force at least once a year.


If you have any questions about the application process, please contact a YWCA associate advocate.
